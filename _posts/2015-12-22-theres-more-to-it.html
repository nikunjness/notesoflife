There's more to it


You'll go by words but there's more to it,
You'll go by expressions but there's more to it,
You'll always complain, this isn't enough,
I will smile and say there's more to it

You'll see the sunshine,
You'll enjoy the view bright,
Flowers will be at peak of their blossom,
view from the window will be always awesome,
still you'll always complain, this isn't enough,
I will smile and say there's more to it

Train journeys will seem boring,
Uncle on the other seat will irritate you with his snoring,
Mountains, rivers, villages and farms will try to make you chill,
I'll ask “why are you still in the mood to kill?”,
You'll always complain, this isn't enough,
I will smile and say there's more to it

No matter how hard you try,
I'm not gonna let you cry,
I know this isn't enough,
there's always more to it, there's always more to it.

- Nikunj Thakkar
